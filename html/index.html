midas provide broadcast and sport equipment purchase services to individuals and corporate organizations as well as organized world soccer tours, trials and tournaments for individual players and teams in africa, europe and the united states.

mission

our mission is to produce the next generation of big stars and talents in the world of football and give them the best education of the game that will last them for the rest of their lives.


vision

our vision is to become the biggest football academy in nigeria and africa, to be the branches and centers in every part of the world, to become the most successful football academy with the type of players that will be produced for national teams and football world

our academy

we believe in numerous human and natural resources that nigeria and africa possess most especially in the football industry and midas football academy was setup to enable us recognize and deploy these talents

with our network of international football clubs and football management organizations around the world, we have been helping young players with opportunities to showcase their talents to football coaches, agents and scouts from all over europae, asia, north africa and the americas. we are committed to the growth of football in nigeria and africa

our strategy

Our Aim Is To Provide The Best Of Facilities, Coaching And Games Programme To Create A Perfect Environment For Players To Develop And Reach Their Full Potentials. We Aim To Develop Players To Play In The First Team Of Different Clubs In Every Part Of The World As Professionals And Our Admission Process Is Rigorous And On A Continuous Basis As More Talented Qualified Students Are Discovered And Invited To Join The Academy.

We Aim To Have A 120 Student's Capacity Hostel For The Junior And Senior Teams In The Nearest Future Where They Will Be 3 Coaches For Both Levels. All Student And Coaches Shall Reside In The Academy Accommodation, Those That Could Not Complete Their High School Education And Those That Are Still In High School Shall Join Academic
